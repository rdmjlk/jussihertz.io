To The Beauty Of Being with Fatäk & Tettix Hexer (2022)

A musical composition made in collaboration with Leila Bélangeon Bouaziz, Aude Barras and Jens Aagaard, released in digital formats on December 2nd, 2022 via Porto-based record label Eastern Nurseries. >
The composition was released with an accompaniment of hand-made silk handkerchief, made by Newcastle upon Tyne -based artist and sculptor Dolores Ramona.
