World Canvas (2020 -)

World Canvas is a curatorial platform which focuses on cultivating the ideas of explorative storytelling via various methods of practice in contemporary art and music. The platform aims to showcase contemporary artists working in the field of creating cross-disciplinary forms of narrative art.
