Romance Relic

Romance Relic is Jussi Hertz's solo artistic alias, focusing on long form audio compositions. Released via Janushoved and DISPLAY, each piece of music reflects on different subject matters ranging from site-specific memory to instrumental mediation of emotion and temporality.
