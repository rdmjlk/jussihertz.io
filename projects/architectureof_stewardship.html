Architecture of Stewardship

The Pavilion – Architecture of Stewardship

Pavilion of Finland At the 19th International Architecture Exhibition of La Biennale di Venezia 10.5.–23.11.2025

The immersive, multi-channel video installation at the Finnish Pavilion of Venice Architecture Biennale 2025 brings to life the memories embedded in the walls of the Pavilion, tracing its history from construction in 1956, through decades of continuous maintenance and three major restorations. The exhibition highlights the often invisible maintenance work and positions the people involved as co-creators alongside the original architect
