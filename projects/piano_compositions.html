Piano Compositions with Murrettumeri (2024)

Piano Compositions album made in collaboration with Murrettumeri (Henia Nikkilä), utilising a range of piano-adjacent VST software and clarinet. Released via Helsinki-based creative platform World Canvas on 23rd of August 2024.

Cover art made by Helsinki-based designer Jassir Kuronen.
